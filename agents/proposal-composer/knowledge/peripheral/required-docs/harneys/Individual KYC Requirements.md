   Ascentium (BVI) Ltd

---
¹ **Reserve Director**: According to the BVI legislation, it is possible to appoint a reserve director when a company has only one individual member and that member is also the sole director of the company. The reserve director will only act in place of the sole director in the event of his/her death. At that time, the reserve director is automatically appointed as full director and is immediately allowed to take on the operation and management of the company.
