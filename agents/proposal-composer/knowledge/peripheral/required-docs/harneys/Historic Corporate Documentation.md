### For the company

1. Copy of the Memorandum and Articles of Association;
2. Copy of the Certificate of Incorporation;
3. Copy of the filed Register of Directors (**IMPORTANT**);
4. Copy of the Register of Members/Shareholders;
5. Copy of Certificate of Incumbency issued within the last 3 months; and
6. Formal confirmation from the current provider that the company is up-to-date with the obligations set forth in the economic substance legislation, having submitted all the applicable reports.

*Please note that we usually request the following documents from the client during the onboarding process:*

7. Certificate of Incorporation;
8. Certificate of Change of Name (if any);
9. Memorandum and Articles of Association including any amendments;
10. Register of Members;
11. Filed Register of Members (if available);
12. Register of Directors;
13. Filed Register of Directors;
14. Filed Beneficial Owner Register (if available);
15. Registers of Officers (if any);
16. Register of Charges (if any);
17. Copy of the seal imprint;
18. Proof of latest Economic Substance reporting filing;
19. Proof of Financial Annual Return filing (if applicable);
20. All resolutions relating to the current changes in directorship, shareholders and officers.
