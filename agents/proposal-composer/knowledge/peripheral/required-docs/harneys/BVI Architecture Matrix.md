### BVI Company

#### Individual Director(s) / Shareholder(s)
* Original certified copy of passport or identity card showing the individual's photo and signature
* Original certified copy of residential address proof issued within last 3 months (e.g. utility bill/bank statement)

#### Corporate Director(s) / Shareholder(s)
* Original certified copy of Certificate of Incorporation ("COI")
* Original certified copy of Memorandum and Articles of Association ("M&A")
* Original certified copy of Register of Directors
* Original certified copy of Register of Members
* Original certified copy of KYC documents of shareholder(s) and Ultimate Beneficial Owner(s) holding more than 10% voting rights

#### Middle Layer Shareholder(s)
* Original certified copy of Register of Members or certified copy of organisation chart.

*\* Please note that original certified KYC document for all directors is essential when incorporating a BVI Company.*
