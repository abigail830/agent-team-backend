## AML Policies and Procedures

We draft and/or review AML Policies and Procedures tailored to the BVI regime. Each entity must adopt an AML manual outlining their policies and procedures. Where an entity places reliance on eligible third-party service providers for specified AML functions, in accordance with BVI AML laws and guidance, a reliance manual is sufficient. Where no reliance is placed on third parties for these services, a full AML manual is required.
