## AEOI Services (CRS only — FATCA Exempt Management Entities)

Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act (FATCA)/Common Reporting Standard (CRS) compliance services are collectively known as Automatic Exchange of Information Services (AEOI Services).

Harneys Fiduciary's Automatic Exchange of Information ("AEOI") Services provide comprehensive FATCA and CRS compliance support for Reporting Financial Institutions in the British Virgin Islands ("BVI"). This service level includes initial registrations, provision of required AEOI officers and portal roles, investor due diligence support, annual report preparation and submission, and administration of statutory portal fees. Fees are based on anticipated reporting complexity in line with guidance issued by the BVI International Tax Authority ("ITA") and relevant OECD standards. Additional charges may apply where non-standard reporting or data remediation is required.

We provide CRS-only AEOI support for eligible FATCA Exempt Management Entities, including classification, onboarding, and ongoing reporting coordination.
