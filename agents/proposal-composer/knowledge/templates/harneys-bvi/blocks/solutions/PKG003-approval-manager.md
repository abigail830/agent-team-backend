## Approved Manager

The BVI Approved Manager regime provides a streamlined route for investment managers to operate from the BVI with a lighter regulatory footprint than a full investment business licence, while maintaining institutional standards.

**Key benefits include:**

- Fast approval pathway for qualifying managers
- Lower ongoing regulatory cost relative to a full licence
- Light-touch ongoing supervision for eligible managers
- Suitable for managers with meaningful assets under management
- Flexibility in structuring investment management activities
- Institutional-grade fiduciary and corporate support from Harneys Fiduciary
- Access to BVI's well-established funds and corporate ecosystem

**Company formation** — We incorporate and maintain the BVI vehicle that will act as Approved Manager, including standard constitutional documents and registry filings.

**Registered agent / registered office** — We provide the mandatory local presence required under BVI law and handle routine registry correspondence.
